A Little About Us

Located in downtown Lincoln Nebraska 1867 bar is a local bar
that is for everyone and welcomes everyone.

WE ARE 1867 BAR

We celebrate everything Nebraska!

Our name refers to the year Nebraska was declared a state. They offer a number of local beers and liquors that come from different local breweries in Nebraska.

We also feature one of a kind music venue that is connected to our bar itself. There is a vast array of all genres performed here not only music but comedy as well.
